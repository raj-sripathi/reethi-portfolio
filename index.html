About

Music that feels like home.

Reethi is an independent artist and musician creating original songs that blend vulnerability with melody. Previously known as Crazy Ramen, Reethi's music spans the spectrum of human emotion — from high-energy tracks like Fire and Bittersweet to intimate solo pieces like paper hearts and imperfectly perfect.

With a growing catalog of originals and carefully chosen covers, every release is crafted to connect — honest lyrics, rich harmonies, and arrangements that breathe.
